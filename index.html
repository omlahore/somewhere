The exit

SOMEWHERE

a door, left open, in the hills
scroll to go through ↓

every door here
opens onto another.

mind the grass —

yes, it's growing indoors.

the kettle's still warm.

you've been here
before. haven't you?

the stairs only go up.

keep climbing —

the sky is
a door too.

↑ the way out is up

You never
really leave
a place like this.

A liminal concept — a door in a field, a house full of grass, a staircase into the sky. Made as an experiment in scroll, sound, and half-remembered rooms.
